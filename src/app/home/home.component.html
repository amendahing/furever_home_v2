Who We Are

Rescuing since 1992

Furever Home is an all-volunteer non-profit Bay Area companion animal rescue organization, comprised entirely of experienced volunteers who collectively have rescued thousands of companion animals and placed them in forever loving homes.


We have 3 main locations in the Bay Area: Oakland, San Jose, and Palo Alto. Come check us out!

Volunteer

Volunteers are vital to operations at the Hayward Animal Shelter. There are many ways you can help us humanely care for and place the more than 3,700 animals that come through the shelter each year.

Spay/Neuter

An estimated 5 million cats and dogs are euthanized in shelters each year. There are not enough homes for all the unwanted pets that are born, so spaying and neutering your pets can alleviate pet overpopulation.
